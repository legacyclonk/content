This is the official Western Objects pack by RedWolf Design.

Object contributions from the Western Objects Design Contest have only been
incorporated from authors who are also team members or official beta testers
of RedWolf Design.

Other objects which have also appeared in the design contest have been
completely rewritten for this pack.

Credit for the original trapper idea in the design contest goes to
Nicolas Harttig (Wipf Hunter).

Credits for the 2013 rework go to Viktor Yuschuk (Viktor).
